The Centro Pi (Projects and Innovation Center IMPA) collaborated with Globo, a prominent entertainment company, to develop an automated metadata extraction and enrichment project using artificial intelligence models. The project aimed to enhance Globo's content recommendation systems by leveraging advanced techniques in data processing and analysis.

The primary objective of the project was to automate the extraction of metadata from a vast collection of movies and TV series provided by Globo. The metadata encompassed various attributes such as genre, themes, technical details, people involved, and depicted locations. By accurately extracting and enriching this metadata, the project sought to improve the content characterization and enhance the precision of Globo's recommendation algorithms.

The project team consisted of researchers, technologists, and scholars from Centro Pi, including Paulo Orenstein, Roberto Imbuzeiro, Roberto Beauclair, Lucas Nissenbaum, Alex Akira, Lucas Resende, Lucas Schwengber, Thiago Ramos, and Rodrigo Schuller. Together, they collaborated closely with Globo's technical teams to understand the requirements, gather relevant data sources, and design the automated metadata extraction pipeline.

The project's workflow involved several key stages. Initially, the team compiled and aggregated multiple metadata sources to create a comprehensive database comprising over 1.5 million movies and TV series. This database served as the foundation for subsequent processing steps. Using advanced machine learning algorithms, the team developed models to extract keywords and other descriptive information from the content, aiming for accuracy and comprehensiveness. They also categorized the extracted metadata into various dimensions, enabling fine-grained characterization of the titles based on genre, themes, and technical attributes.

To ensure data accuracy and quality, the team implemented data cleansing and validation techniques to address common aggregation errors and inconsistencies. They focused on resolving challenges such as disambiguating different movies that share similar attributes and aligning metadata across different sources.

The project's outcome was a robust and interpretable recommendation system capable of providing accurate content suggestions to Globo's audience. By leveraging the enriched metadata and advanced algorithms, the system could generate personalized recommendations based on users' preferences and browsing history.

Throughout the project, the team collaborated closely with Globo's stakeholders, regularly presenting progress updates and gathering feedback to refine the system's performance. The project not only aimed to enhance Globo's content recommendation capabilities but also fostered research and innovation in the field of automated metadata extraction and enrichment.

Overall, the automated metadata extraction and enrichment project served as a significant technological advancement for Globo, empowering the company to deliver more personalized and engaging content recommendations to its vast audience. The collaboration between Centro Pi and Globo demonstrated the successful application of advanced artificial intelligence techniques in the entertainment industry, paving the way for further advancements in content recommendation systems.
