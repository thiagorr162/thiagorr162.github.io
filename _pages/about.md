I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Statistics at the Federal University of São Carlos (UFSCar), Brazil. I earned my Ph.D. in Mathematics from the Instituto de Matemática Pura e Aplicada (IMPA) in Rio de Janeiro, where my thesis focused on boosting and concentration of measure methods in machine learning. I completed my M.Sc. in Mathematics at the Universidade de São Paulo (ICMC-USP), with a dissertation on ergodic theory in homogeneous flows and Ratner's theorems. I also hold a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics from the same institution.
