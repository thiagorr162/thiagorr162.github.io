I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the Center for Projects and Innovation at IMPA. My educational background includes a bachelor's and master's degree in pure mathematics from the University of São Paulo, as well as a Ph.D. at IMPA, where I was supervised by Roberto I. Oliveira and Paulo Orentein. My research interests lie in the field of theoretical and applied machine learning, particularly in the areas of booting, conformal prediction, and learning to hash. I also have experience in applying machine learning to industrial settings, having worked with companies such as Cartesi, Stone Pagamentos, DASA, and Rede Globo.
